ABOUT US

Flaming feast was born in 2020 after a fruitless search for a good old-fashioned hamburger. During their days in the financial industry, Wilbert and Gerald traveled the world and discovered that the best food, anywhere in the world, could be found in restaurants that had a limited menu. Back home in Oslob,Cebu the inability to find a well-made classic hamburger was particularly frustrating to them. Despite Oslob being a hub for restaurants, and hamburgers, They simply could not find the delicious old-fashioned fresh burger they were seeking. It felt as though restaurants were trying too hard to be different and innovative, overcomplicating a simple concept.

As our Flaming feast fandom grows, so does our company. Our Flaming feast fans know what they like, and they keep coming back for more. Since our first store opened at Poblacion, Oslob, Cebu, our customers' loyalty has only increased! We know what our customers want, and we promise to continue to deliver! We know that our greatness is in our 100% fresh and filler-free Canadian Angus Beef. And we will never cut corners to save on cost. The bottom line? Our burgers Taste Good like a Hamburger Should!
